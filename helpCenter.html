    • Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I make a reservation?

    Making a reservation is quick and easy. Simply visit our website, choose the desired location, date, and time, select the car you want, and proceed to book it. You can also give us a call, and our friendly customer support team will assist you with the reservation process.

    What documents do I need to rent a car?

    To rent a car, you will need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name for the rental payment, and a form of identification, such as a passport or national ID card. The specific requirements may vary depending on the country and rental policies, so it's best to check with us or refer to the terms and conditions.

    Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

    Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation. Simply contact our customer support team or log in to your account on our website and manage your booking. Please note that there may be certain terms and conditions regarding modifications or cancellations, so it's advisable to review our policies or contact us for assistance.

    What should I do in case of an accident or breakdown?

    In case of an accident or breakdown, your safety is our top priority. Please contact our emergency helpline immediately, and we will guide you through the necessary steps. We have roadside assistance services available to ensure you receive prompt support and necessary arrangements for your safety and convenience.
